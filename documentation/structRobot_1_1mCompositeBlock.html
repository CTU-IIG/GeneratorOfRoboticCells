Detailed Description

The structure containing the activities in a block.

It integrates activities with their interconnections into an easy-to-connect block, which can be e.g. cutting block, assembling block, welding block, etc. The structure contains the various types of activities: in ~ IN, w ~ INNER, out ~ OUT, mv ~ MOVEMENT, con1 ~ IN*, con2 ~ OUT*. Activities in con1 and con2 are inputs and outputs of the block respectively.
